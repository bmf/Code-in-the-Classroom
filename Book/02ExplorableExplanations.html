Algorithms

Here's one of my favorite algorithms:

Eskimo Snowballs

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp. water
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 3 tbsp. cocoa
  • 2 cups oatmeal
  • Confectioners' sugar

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar together.
  2. Mix in water, vanilla and cocoa.
  3. Stir in oatmeal until well mixed. Refrigerate overnight.
  4. Shape into balls. Roll in confectioners' sugar. Store in refrigerator.
