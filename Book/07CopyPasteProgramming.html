One of the things I've been pushing is reading. I think that is the most useful thing that a community of programmers can do for each other—spend time on a regular basis reading each other's code. Internet-Access as an Ethical Issue

The science fiction author William Gibson is often credited with observing, "The future is already here — it’s just not very evenly distributed."

Internet access is a moral issue for the Information Age. We should have 7 billion minds online. Not only is it wrong to deprive nearly 4 Billion of our fellow human beings the enducational and enlightenment opportunities www access provides, but we are also depriving ourselves of the additional unique perspectives, -----, and sheer brain power that will come with putting the whole world online.
